V/Line

V/Line trains and coaches can take you to all pockets of regional Victroria. You can connect with coach services at many points along the major train lines to travel to destinations throughout the state.

High frequency trains and coaches run from Melbourne to Geelong (south west), Ballarat (west), Bendigo (north), Seymour (north east) and Gippsland (east).

Intercity services also provide links between major provincial cities.
